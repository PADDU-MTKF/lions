Activities

We have regular classrooms where children are taught to read and write in English and Kannada. Classrooms are split into pre-primary, primary, secondary, pre-vocational, vocational sections. And students are placed in different section depending on their mental ability and IQ.

Physical exercises and yoga are part of daily routine. Physiotherapy and speech therapy treatments are regularly given to needy children. We counsel children regularly.
