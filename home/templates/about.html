Infrastructure

The present school is 3450 sq. ft and includes 12 classrooms, hall an area for yoga and exercise, food court, conference hall, Physiotherapy, Psychology and Psychiatry room, and an office for the Accountant and Head Mistress.

With the growing number of students, (72 at present), we need to expand our facilities to improve the quality of life of our children.

Though we receive grant from Karnataka State government, we are falling short despite using the entire backup reserve from our school funds. The Lions Club Surathkal members are generous enough to donate from their own pockets. The generosity of kind hearted people like you will add to our effort and boost the functioning of the school.

This school project is a need-based humanitarian activities and an ambitious project, but we believe, it can function with support of generous corporates and philanthropists.

Activities

We have regular classrooms where children are taught to read and write in English. Classrooms are split into pre-primary, primary, secondary, pre-vocational, vocational sections. And students are placed in different section depending on their mental ability and IQ.

Physical exercises and yoga are part of daily routine. Physiotherapy and speech therapy treatments are regularly given to needy children. We counsel children regularly.
