School Facilities/activities

Our school provides tailored English literacy instruction alongside physical activities, yoga, and regular therapies and counseling sessions, fostering holistic development for students of varying abilities across different age groups.

Customized Education:

Students are placed in sections based on their mental abilities, ensuring personalized learning experiences.

Health and Wellness:

Daily physical exercises, yoga, and therapy sessions promote both physical and mental well-being.

Regular Counseling:

Ongoing counseling supports students' emotional and academic needs.

Individualized Support:

Each student receives tailored attention to thrive academically, emotionally, and physically.

Our Objectives

To help and train these special children to become self-dependent and be able to take up personal responsibilities without help from their parents or other relatives.

Our Goals

To bring the special children into the main stream so that they can support themselves and are financially independent.
